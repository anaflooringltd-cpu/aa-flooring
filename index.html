What we do

Four things, done properly.

We don't carpet, we don't tile. We focus on the surfaces we know inside-out, and we make the trim and stairs ourselves so the job comes out of one shop.

Hardwood Flooring

Solid & Engineered

We install both solid and engineered hardwood — species ranging from white oak and maple to acacia, teak, and walnut. Pre-finished or site-finished depending on what the project calls for.

Every install starts with a subfloor check. If the slab isn't flat or the moisture content is off, we sort that first. A floor is only as good as what's under it.

We also do herringbone and other pattern work for clients who want something that stands out. Adds time, not drama.

Vinyl & Laminate

Click-Lock & Glue-Down

LVP (luxury vinyl plank) is fully waterproof and handles the kind of abuse hardwood can't — pet claws, spills, basement moisture. We install both click-lock and glue-down formats depending on the subfloor situation.

Laminate is a smart choice for basement suites, rental units, and high-traffic areas where you want a wood look at a lower price point. We supply acoustic-rated underlay on every strata install.

Custom Accessories

Milled In-Shop

Transitions, reducers, T-moldings, quarter-round, and threshold pieces — milled in our Coquitlam shop to match whatever you're putting on the floor. Off-the-shelf trim almost never fits right.

If the floor crosses a height change or meets tile or carpet, we make an accessory that actually works. Matching species, matching stain, clean fit.

Stair Treads & Casings

Custom Fabricated

This is the work we're probably best known for. We build stair treads, risers, and casings to fit — curved stairs, open-sided stairs, waterfall edges, floating configurations. All fabricated in-shop from the same species as your floor.

A mismatched staircase undoes a beautiful floor. We don't let that happen.

Where we work

Lower Mainland, BC

Based at 629 Smith Ave in Coquitlam, we work within about 40 km of the shop — the Tri-Cities, Burnaby, New West, and Vancouver.

Coquitlam

Our home base. We know the neighbourhoods here — Burquitlam, Burke Mountain, Westwood Plateau — and have done dozens of projects across the city. Short drive from the shop means we can respond quickly and supply extra materials same day if needed.

Port Moody

Lots of character homes in Port Moody and Inlet Centre condos. We're comfortable with both — older subfloors that need levelling, and modern slab construction. Just east of the shop.

Burnaby

From Metrotown highrises to Burnaby Heights character houses. We handle strata work with acoustic-rated underlay to keep building management happy, and we're familiar with the older building stock in East Burnaby.

Vancouver

East Van, Hastings-Sunrise, Commercial Drive, Kitsilano — wherever the project is. We do a few Vancouver projects each month, usually hardwood in character homes or stair work that follows a floor install.

About

A small shop that does one thing well.

A&A Flooring opened its doors in 2016 at 629 Smith Ave in Coquitlam. We're a hands-on, owner-operated shop — the same people who quote your job are the ones who show up on day one with a measuring tape.

We focus on hardwood, vinyl, laminate, and the trim and stairs that go with them. We don't do tile, we don't do carpet, and we don't subcontract our installs. If you booked us, you got us.

Most of our work happens within 40 km of the shop — the Tri-Cities, Burnaby, New West, East Van — though we do a little further afield for projects that match our wheelhouse.
